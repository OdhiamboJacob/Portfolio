WHO IS JACK?

A proactive and detail-oriented Geoinformatics practitioner with a robust foundation developed through five years of study at Taita Taveta University and over 4 years of hands on experience in GIS, Remote Sensing, and water resource management. My career highlights include impactful contributions to Siaya Bondo Water and Sanitation Company (SIBOWASCO) and freelance GIS projects. I excel in applying geospatial tools and software to address real-world challenges, including data collection, cleaning, analyzing, Visualizing, Presenting patterns, and system optimization. Notable accomplishments include designing interactive legend for tracking meter readings, developing and implementing realtime reporting systems, and mapping extensive water resource facilities for efficient planning and operations. I have also contributed to sustainable development projects, such as proposing smart-city solutions for Bondo municipality to improve water availability and waste management. My work demonstrates my strong commitment to delivering quality outputs that align with personal goals, organizational goals and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). I am a natural problem solver, highly self-motivated, dedicated to teamwork and continuous learning, ensuring that I bring value and innovation to every project I undertake.
